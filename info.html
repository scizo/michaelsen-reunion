It all began at Aunt LaRue’s in 1949 during a birthday party for Grandma Ella. Ella was concerned that her large family, then living in Utah, Wyoming and California, would not get together regularly. At that moment they agreed to come together each August to keep the family bonds strong and the love alive. And it worked!

2012 marks the 63rd annual Michaelsen Family reunion in Mayfield, Utah -- known to many of us as simply “The Mayfield Reunion.” This year we will welcome over 250 family members–four generations–from all over the nation. We’ll serve over 1200 meals, eat more treats than we'll admit, tell stories, reconnect with family members and make new friends as we celebrate a remarkable and long legacy of what it means to be a family!

0 - 5: free, 6 - 12: $10, 13+: $18

Memorial

This year we would like to pay tribute to our loved ones who have gone before us by creating a simple memorial area. Please feel free to bring a picture, small object, poem, etc. that will allow us all a moment to remember and reflect on those who made us who we are but are no longer with us.
